4In this step, you are evaluating the uncertainty of the point forecasts . That is, you are inspecting
the prediction intervals of the point forecasts. A good forecasting model should not only provide accurate
point forecasts but also small and reliable prediction intervals. Note: The point forecast accuracy tells you
how well the model performs with the specific sample you are using. The point forecast uncertainty (the
prediction intervals) provides you with an estimate of how well it performs on new samples on average.
